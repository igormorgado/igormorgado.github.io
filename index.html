Hi welcome the singularity!

Pierre de Fermat was a mathematician and scientist. Most famous for what he didn't did, the "Fermat's last therorem". He has written aside in a page the following statement.

"There aren't any set of positive integers x, y, z and n with n bigger than 2 that satisfies the equation".

xn + yn = zn

And me? I did nothing. But you can check my workings in my GitHub.The ones I'm most proud of are (no special order):

  • numerico: Codes about numerical analysis and methods. In Python.
  • wave: Wave Phenomena simulation. In C
  • Seismic: Seismic simulations. In Python
  • assetsbrowser: A browser for game assets. In C/Python
  • pong: A clone of an very old game. In C.
  • factory: A tool to manage source compilation. In Bash.
  • liblohs: A driver for optical markers (very old). In C.

That is it.
